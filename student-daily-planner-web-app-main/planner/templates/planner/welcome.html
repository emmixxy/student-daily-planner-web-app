About Daily Planner

Plan your study and activities efficiently. Set your weekly class hours, choose activities, and auto-generate a timetable you can print or sync to your calendar.

How to use

  1. Set your weekly class times on the Preferences page.
  2. Select up to 10 activities on the Activities page.
  3. On the Timetable, click Fill Activities to distribute them, edit cells as needed, and save or export.

Tips

  • Use Grey Edit to note class details directly in grey cells.
  • Save as PDF for printing, or Sync to Google to export ICS.
